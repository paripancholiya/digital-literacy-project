Do's
-> Do keep your privacy settings updated to control who sees your personal posts.
->Do use social media to showcase your academic projects and technical skills.
->Do verify the source of information or news before sharing or reposting it.
->Do connect with alumni and industry professionals to build a career network.
->Do think for ten seconds before hitting 'post' to ensure your message is clear.

Don'ts
->Don't post confidential information about your college, exams, or peers.
->Don't share your live location or private contact details on public platforms.
->Don't vent about professors, colleagues, or your institution on public feeds.
->Don't use offensive language or engage in heated arguments (flaming) online.
->Don't post photos or videos that could be perceived as unprofessional by recruiters.
