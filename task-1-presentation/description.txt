Project Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic 

Tool Used: Canva.

Description: This infographic was designed to help my peers understand the core meaning of digital literacy and why it is essential for students. The visual covers three main topics: an overview of digital literacy, a list of safe internet practices (such as using 2FA and recognizing phishing), and the importance of maintaining a professional online presence on platforms like LinkedIn.

Goal: As a Student Digital Ambassador, my goal is to provide a one-page visual resource that makes complex digital safety topics accessible and engaging for my batchmates.
